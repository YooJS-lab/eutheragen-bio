About

A biotech company built from first-principles immunobiology.

Eutheragen Bio is advancing a new therapeutic approach to severe inflammatory disease by targeting the proximal molecular circuitry that drives immune escalation rather than merely suppressing downstream consequences.

Our company is founded on deep mechanistic expertise in immunology, virology, and host-pathogen biology, and is focused on translating differentiated biological insight into strategically valuable therapeutic assets.

Scientific thesis

Why MCEMP1 matters

We believe MCEMP1 is not merely associated with inflammation. It may act as an upstream driver of inflammatory amplification and a tractable therapeutic intervention node.
