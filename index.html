SCIENTIFIC FOUNDATION

Built on mechanistic immunology, virology, and translational precision.

Eutheragen Bio is rooted in high-resolution discovery science. Our core thesis is simple: breakthrough therapeutics emerge when disease-driving immune circuitry is mapped with enough depth to reveal tractable intervention nodes.

Novel inflammatory receptor axis

Our anti-inflammatory program is anchored in MCEMP1, a newly defined immune receptor implicated in amplifying pathologic inflammation in pulmonary and blood-associated settings. This provides a distinctive entry point beyond conventional broad-spectrum immunosuppression.

Host–virus interaction intelligence

Our antiviral discovery engine is informed by direct expertise in how viruses suppress, evade, and reprogram host immunity. This enables more selective intervention design at the host-pathogen interface, where durable leverage is often found.

Platform-oriented therapeutic logic

Rather than pursuing isolated assets alone, we build modular discovery capabilities that can generate multiple candidates across inflammatory and viral disease categories, strengthening both pipeline resilience and strategic partnering value.

PIPELINE VISION

Two science engines. Multiple value-creation pathways.

PLATFORM 01

MCEMP1-targeted anti-inflammatory therapeutics

A first-in-class opportunity built on suppressing both the activity and gene expression of MCEMP1 to attenuate disease-driving inflammatory amplification.

  • Mechanism-centered approach targeting a newly defined inflammatory receptor axis.
  • Therapeutic rationale spans lung inflammation, systemic inflammatory conditions, and blood-associated inflammatory pathology.
  • Potential to move beyond non-specific anti-inflammatory control toward biomarker-informed precision intervention.
  • Applicable to small molecules, biologics, nucleic acid modalities, and expression-control strategies.
PLATFORM 02

Antiviral, anti-inflammatory, and vaccine-enabling programs for emerging viruses

A discovery platform powered by proprietary systems that interrogate host immune suppression and viral immune evasion, enabling faster target definition against present and future viral threats.

  • Focus areas include SARS-CoV-2, Nipah virus, influenza virus, Zika virus, dengue virus, and future outbreak-prone pathogens.
  • Designed to uncover intervention nodes that support both antiviral efficacy and pathologic inflammation control.
  • Flexible downstream paths: therapeutics, host-directed countermeasures, adjuvant-enabling systems, and vaccine candidate support.
  • Scalable scientific value through reusable assay systems and pathogen-agnostic host-biology insights.
